# Code of Conduct

We keep this project collaborative and professional.

## Expected behavior

- Respect the purpose of our community, our activities, and our ways of gathering.
- Engage kindly and honestly with others.
- Respect different viewpoints and experiences.
- Take responsibility for your actions and contributions.
- Give and accept constructive feedback.
- Commit to repairing harm when it occurs.
- Behave in ways that promote and sustain the well-being of our community.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Character attacks: insulting, demeaning, or pejorative comments directed at a person or group.
- Failing to credit sources when contributing content.
- Violating confidentiality: sharing or acting on private information without permission.
- Other behavior that threatens the well-being of our community.

## Scope

Applies to all project spaces (issues, pull requests, discussions, and other community channels).

## Reporting

Open an issue to report violations. If sensitive, request a private contact channel.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit/remove content, lock threads, or restrict participation.
